My Background

Hello! I'm Thomas Peacock, a Service Desk Manager at NHS Tayside. With a passion for IT service management and first-line technical support, I specialise in ensuring seamless incident resolution and technical assistance for healthcare professionals.

My role involves leading a high-performing IT service desk team, optimising service delivery, and implementing process improvements to enhance operational efficiency in a high-pressure NHS environment. I am committed to fostering a culture of continuous improvement while ensuring that critical IT services remain reliable and effective for healthcare professionals.

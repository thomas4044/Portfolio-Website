My Background

Hello! I'm Thomas Peacock, a Service Desk Manager at NHS Tayside. With a passion for IT service management and first-line technical support, I specialise in ensuring seamless incident resolution and technical assistance for healthcare professionals.

My role involves leading a high-performing IT service desk team, optimising service delivery, and implementing process improvements to enhance operational efficiency in a high-pressure NHS environment. I am committed to fostering a culture of continuous improvement while ensuring that critical IT services remain reliable and effective for healthcare professionals.

Education

  • Bachelor of Science in IT Management for Business - University of Dundee (2028)
  • Bachelor of Science in Materials Chemistry - University of St Andrews (2023)
  • ITIL4 Foundation - QA Training (2023)

My Interests

Outside of work, I am an avid Scuba Diver! I am a Dive Leader and Assistant Diving Instructor qualified under the British Sub-Aqua Club!

I spend most of my time scuba diving around Scotland, on both the East and West coasts! I am actively involved with the Cupar Sub-Aqua Club, a branch of BSAC. I am the elected diving officer, and sit on the club committee. However, I also dive with other clubs around Scotland. Below is a gallery of photos and videos from some of my dives!
